FeedCaching

public protocol FeedCaching

A persistent cache for feeds and entries.

  • update(feeds:)

    Updates feeds in the cache, while new feeds are inserted.

    Before insertion, to avoid doublets, feeds with matching iTunes GUIDs are removed. These doublets may occure, after the feed URL has changed while the iTunes GUID stayed the same. Just inserting such a feed would result in a unique constraint failure in the database.

    Throws

    Skull errors originating from SQLite.
